How DocSift Pro activates

DocSift Pro activates automatically after checkout. No activation codes, and nothing to paste into the app. Here’s exactly how it works:

1

Purchase DocSift Pro

Click any Get DocSift Pro button on this page to go to our secure Stripe checkout. Complete your purchase using your preferred payment method.

2

Automatic account upgrade

After checkout, Stripe notifies DocSift instantly. Your account is upgraded to Pro automatically—no codes, no manual steps.

3

Open the DocSift app

Click Open App at the top of this site (or go to docsift.streamlit.app) while logged in with the same email you used at checkout. The app will detect your Pro status immediately.

4

Enjoy Pro features instantly

When Pro is active, the sidebar will show ⭐ DocSift Pro, and all Pro-only tools—Key Points, Action Items, Risk Analysis, Explain Like I'm 12, Clarity Rewrites, Study Guides, and Full Reports—unlock automatically.

If your Pro status doesn’t appear after checkout, just contact support@getdocsift.com and we'll help right away.
